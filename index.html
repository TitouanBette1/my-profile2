More About Us

We are a website for presentation and introduction on skateboarding. Our goal is to discover skateboarding which is a little known discipline but very interesting

The skateboard is the essential object. It is possible to customize it to infinity, whether the wheels, the grip or the underside.

Skateboarding is a community sport that is practiced in a skatepark open to all and most of the time outside.

it is also a competitive sport that is the subject of many competitions and has recently been added to the Olympic Games
